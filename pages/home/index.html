Our Mission

Bridging the Tech Opportunity Gap

We believe that talent is universal but opportunity is not. CodeBridge Foundation exists to level the playing field — providing underrepresented communities with world-class technical education, mentorship from industry professionals, and direct pathways into rewarding tech careers.

◆
Accessibility First
All programs are free or subsidized for qualifying participants.
